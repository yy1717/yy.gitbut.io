About MindRead University

Unlock the human mind. Pursue the truth.

Founded in 2025 and located in Desa Cemerlang, Malaysia.

MindRead University is a pioneering institution specializing in the field of psychology with a strong emphasis on criminal investigation training.

In a country where fields such as criminal psychology and behavioral profiling are still underrepresented, MindRead University aims to nurture skilled investigators who can contribute to solving real-life cases across regions.

Beyond academic knowledge, MindRead University also committed to analyzing the deeper motives of criminals behind offenders and individuals with mental illnesses.

Join MindRead University to learn and explore more psychology knowledge!
